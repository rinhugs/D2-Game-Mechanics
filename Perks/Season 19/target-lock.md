# TARGET LOCK

Applies stacking buff to weapon damage, while continuously damaging target.

Buff increases over time, with maximum of 5 stacks.

~25% = Average Damage Buff after firing 100% of magazine.

Damage Buff Per Stack:
* 1 Stack = 16.73% 
* 2 Stacks = 23.47% 
* 3 Stacks = 29.28% 
* 4 Stacks = 34.73% 
* 5 Stacks = 40%
