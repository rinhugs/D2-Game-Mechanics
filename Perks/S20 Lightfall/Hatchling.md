# Hatchling
## TLDR
Precision kills\* with this weapon spawns a Threadling.  

For Non-Precision Weapons, rapid kills spawn a Threadling.  

(Note: Auto Rifles, SMGs, Hand Cannons, and Snipers spawn Threadlings only on precision kills.)
